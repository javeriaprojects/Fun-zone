🐉 The Dragon’s Library

Deep in the misty peaks of Echo Mountain, a wise dragon named Ember stumbled upon a crumbling stone tower. Inside, he found a forgotten library filled with books that shimmered like stars and fluttered their pages like wings.

Each night, Ember would light a candle with his fiery breath and read tales aloud to the woodland creatures. Rabbits, owls, and even the shy wolves gathered near to hear legends of floating castles and invisible pirates.

One day, Ember discovered a blank book whose pages would write themselves. Whatever he imagined would appear in glowing ink—his own stories of kindness and courage, now part of the enchanted shelves forever.

He spent the rest of his days as the Mountain’s storyteller. His favorite tale was the one that began, “Once upon a time, a dragon found a magical library…”
